AI Gateway

Control, cache, and monitor your AI applications. Get visibility into your AI traffic with analytics, rate limiting, and cost-saving caching.

What is AI Gateway?

AI Gateway sits between your application and AI providers (like OpenAI, Anthropic, or Workers AI), giving you complete visibility and control over your AI traffic.

Key Features

  • Caching - Cache identical requests to reduce costs and latency
  • Rate Limiting - Control request rates to prevent abuse
  • Analytics - Track usage, costs, and performance
  • Logging - Log all requests for debugging and compliance
  • Fallbacks - Automatic failover to backup providers

Supported Providers

OpenAI Anthropic Workers AI Azure OpenAI HuggingFace
